Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course hands-on?

Yes, the course is highly practical with numerous exercises, labs, and a capstone project to solidify your learning.

What's the difference between Data Science and Data Analytics?

Data Analytics focuses on understanding past and present data, while Data Science often involves predicting future trends and building more complex models. This course provides a strong foundation for both.

Will I get access to the software?

We guide you through setting up free versions of necessary software (like Python distributions and Tableau Public) or provide access where applicable.
